At a Glance

Etymology
Icelandic líð (passage of time, slipping away) → drifted to Lioaru
Position
Southwest Talan, south of Sumendar, west of Brauogi and Ezkudon, coastal on Hafra to the south
Terrain
Desert dominant with orange sand dunes throughout. A green river valley cuts through as an oasis of life. The dark, shadowy Lost Kingdom occupies the western coastal area. Lost Isle sits offshore. Temporal anomalies throughout: moments that repeat, ruins newer than they should be.
Character
Desert, temporal anomalies, ancient ruins
