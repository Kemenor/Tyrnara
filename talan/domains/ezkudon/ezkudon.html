Ezkudon
Knowledge Domain
Bound to Enki
Knowledge here is collected, preserved, and controlled. Access is negotiated, not assumed.
✦

At a Glance

Etymology
Basque ezkutu (hidden, concealed) → drifted to Ezkudon
Position
South-central Talan, below Brauogi, west of Egulon, north of Hafra
Terrain
Distinctive circular ring of forest and hills with mountains at the centre — like a natural fortress grown around the great archives. Green forest in the north and west, sandy desert in the south, coastal access southeast, lakes to the west.
Bound God
Enki — domain of Knowledge
God's City
Thekkavar
The City of Learning
