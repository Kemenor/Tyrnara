History
The Eras of Talan
All dates use the two-calendar system anchored at the Week of Crimson Rain (0 GR / 0 MR). Before it: GR (Gods Reign), counting down. After: MR (Mortal Reign), counting up. Current year: 2532 MR.
✦

Eras at a Glance

Creation
6001 GR
The beginning of recorded possibility. The Wellspring’s outpouring gives rise to Tyrnarra. The twelve Grand Gods are present and active from the first moment.
Elden Era
6000 GR — 2945 GR
Three thousand years dominated by the Elden — neither fully mortal nor fully divine. Created the Android ancestry. Vanished on a single day in 2945 GR.
Gods’ Era
2944 GR — 1 GR
Three thousand years of direct divine rule. Gods as kings, gods as landlords, gods as law. Prosperity and petty divine warfare in equal measure.
Week of Crimson Rain
0 GR — 0 MR
The pivotal week. Skies wept divine blood. At the end, the Gods’ Law was forged — thirteen accepted its binding and stayed; the rest withdrew.
Lost Era
1 MR — 559 MR
Five centuries of mortal civilisation learning to govern itself for the first time. Fragmentation, rediscovery, and immense loss of knowledge.
Golden Era
560 MR — 1325 MR
The Golden Empire — a dwarven empire controlling roughly 70% of Talan. The largest mortal empire in recorded history. Produced the Automaton ancestry.
Dark Era & the Guild’s Birth
1321 MR — 2135 MR
Miners breached the sealed Elden ritual site. The Corrupted God emerged. Eight centuries of attrition. The Adventurers Guild rose from conscience. Defeated in 2135 MR.
Adventurer Era
2135 MR — Present (2532 MR)
Four centuries of rebuilding and uneasy opportunity. The Adventurers Guild has become a world-spanning institution of soft power. Spawn still emerge from deep places, but the world is alive again.
