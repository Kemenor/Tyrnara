Origin

The Corrupted God was defeated in 2135 MR but not destroyed. Its spawn still emerge from deep places. Certain ruins, certain mining operations that go too deep, certain sealed Elden sites — all carry risk of emergence.

✦

Current state

Frequency of emergence events has dropped dramatically over four centuries but never reached zero. The spawn vary enormously — some mindless, some disturbingly intelligent, all hostile. The Adventurers Guild maintains a standing research operation to understand and predict emergence patterns. Some researchers believe the Corrupted God is healing.

✦

True nature

Not common knowledge. See world-notes (GM only) for the actual origin of the Corrupted God and what was breached in 1321 MR. Not bound by the Gods' Law — predates it, was never party to it.
