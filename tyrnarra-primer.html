The World

Tyrnarra is a world adrift in an endless ocean, its lands ringed by the still, white expanse of the Cloud Sea. Above it, the heavens turn with the slow rhythm of three celestial bodies — the golden sun Solyra, and two moons, Veyru and Calune — each day and night sinking into, and rising from, the Cloud Sea's horizon.

The Cloud Sea
An infinite, unbroken plain of luminous white vapor. Never shifts with wind, tide, or season. Ships that are not built for its surface slowly sink without trace. Only cloudships can cross it.
The Celestial Descent
All three bodies rise and set from the Cloud Sea, vanishing into its depths each cycle and returning from the opposite horizon — as if passing into another realm beneath.
The Continent of Talan
The great landmass at the heart of the known world. Home to the 13 bound gods of the Material Plane and the kingdoms, city-states, and contested territories shaped by their domains.
Cloudships
Vessels specially built to traverse the Cloud Sea. Rare, expensive, and strategically invaluable — the difference between a regional power and a world power.

The Wellspring

The Wellspring is not merely a source — it is the first place. It is its own plane of existence, from which all other planes spring forth: material, spiritual, and elemental alike.

Every living thing, every plane, and even the stars themselves trace their origin to the Wellspring. In time, all that exists will return to it, dissolving back into the primal flow from which it came. Tyrnarra is one of countless worlds born of its outpouring — a single leaf on an infinite, ever-growing tree.

All of reality is arranged into three great layers, each containing three primary planes. The layers flow in an eternal cycle, as the Wellspring sends out creation and calls it home.

Transitional Veil: The Ethereal Plane (The Invocation of Gods' Law)

A shimmering membrane around the Life Layer. A place where divine will and cosmic law shape mortal reality — and where the 13 bound gods operate under the Gods' Law.

    The plane of Chaos. Vibrant, shifting, and unpredictable.

The 13 Bound Gods

Thirteen major deities reside on the Material Plane, bound by the Gods' Law to operate within its rules. Unlike gods of Layer 1 or Layer 3, these gods walk among mortals — but are constrained by the Ethereal Veil's divine compact. Their domains define the regions of Talan itself.
