✦ The Five-Year Conclave ✦
Once every five years, all thirteen meet in Frae City — neutral ground, Cronus's domain, the only place none of them can claim as home territory. What is decided there is rarely announced. Kingdoms send observers. Scholars study the outcomes for years after. The gods arrive without ceremony and leave the same way. Cronus is always already there when they arrive.
