# **Tyrnarra**
Tyrnarra is a world adrift in an endless ocean, its lands ringed by the still, white expanse of the **Cloud Sea**. Above it, the heavens turn with the slow rhythm of three celestial bodies — a golden sun and two moons — each day and night sinking into, and rising from, the Cloud Sea’s horizon.

---

## **The Cloud Sea**

- **Nature:** An infinite, unbroken plain of luminous, snow-white vapor. It never shifts with wind, tide, or season.
- **Peril:** Ships and airships that are not built for its strange surface will slowly sink, vanishing without trace. Only **cloudships** can cross it.
- **Mystery:** The Cloud Sea swallows the sun and moons each cycle, returning them on the opposite horizon. Some say it is the veil between all worlds.

---

## **The Celestial Descent**

- **The Sun – Solyra:** A blazing disc of gold, warming the world before sinking into the Cloud Sea at dusk.
- **The Red Moon – Veyru:** Deep crimson, its light seems to shimmer faintly in motion.
- **The Green Moon – Calune:** Pale jade, with marbled streaks that shift subtly over the seasons.
- All three rise and set from the Cloud Sea, vanishing into its depths as if passing into another realm.

---

## **The Wellspring**

- **The Wellspring** is not merely a source — it is the **first place**.
- It is its own plane of existence, from which all other planes — material, spiritual, and elemental — spring forth.
- Every living thing, every plane, and even the stars themselves trace their origin to the Wellspring.
- In time, all that exists will return to it, dissolving back into the primal flow from which it came.
- Tyrnarra is one of countless worlds born of its outpouring, a single leaf on an infinite, ever-growing tree.
