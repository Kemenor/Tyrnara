**Araphel (The God of Shadows, Forgotten Things, and New Faces)**

NOTE: NOT  B

**Alignment:** Neutral (N)

**Pantheon/Region:** Revered throughout Araphel, centered in Myrria

**Areas of Concern:** Darkness, shadows, forgetting, rebirth, second chances

**Follower Alignments:** LG, NG, CG, LN, N, CN

**Edicts**

- Offer sanctuary to those who seek a new life
- Conceal the secrets of those who entrust them to you
- Protect those who flee past dangers or persecution
- Embrace change, taking on new names, faces, or roles as needed

**Anathema**

- Expose a secret entrusted to you
- Deny someone the chance to atone or begin anew
- Hunt those who have renounced their past under Araphel’s shadow
- Refuse to adapt to change when change is needed

**Divine Font**

**Harm or Heal** (depending on the cleric; both are common)

**Divine Skill**

**Stealth**

**Favored Weapon**

**Dagger**

**Domains**

**Darkness, Secrecy, Change, Repose**

**Alternate Domains (GM’s discretion)**

**Trickery, Void**

**Symbol**

A half-mask, split down the middle — one side smooth and featureless, the other etched with intricate, shifting patterns.

**Sacred Colors**

Deep violet, black, and silver

**Sacred Animal**

**Raven** — a creature of both shadow and memory, often depicted carrying a lantern or veil.

**Avatar (Divine Intercession)**

When Araphel manifests, they appear in many faces — male, female, or otherwise — but always at the age of thirty-five. Their avatars are draped in veils of shadow, and their eyes glimmer silver in the dark.

**Araphel — Divine Intercession**

**Minor Boon**

You may fade into shadow when needed. Once per day, you can cast **invisibility** as a divine innate spell.

**Moderate Boon**

Araphel grants you the chance to walk unnoticed, forgotten by those who would bind you to the past. Whenever you attempt a **Deception** check to Lie or a **Stealth** check to Hide, roll twice and take the better result.

**Major Boon**

Araphel remakes you with a new face. You can cast **humanoid form** at will as a divine innate spell, choosing any ancestry and appearance each time. Those who knew you before cannot recognize you unless you allow it. This effect is permanent until dismissed, though you may return to your true form at will.

**Minor Curse**

Your past clings to you like a shadow. Whenever you attempt to **Lie** or **Conceal an Object**, you roll twice and take the worse result.

**Moderate Curse**

The god withdraws their shadow from you, leaving you painfully exposed. You lose the ability to benefit from **concealment** or **cover** against attacks; enemies always seem to find you in the open.

**Major Curse**

Araphel strips you of the safety of the forgotten. Everyone you meet recalls you vividly — you cannot hide or disguise yourself by any means, mundane or magical. Even those who have never seen you before recognize you instantly and recall every detail of your presence.
