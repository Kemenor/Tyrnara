**Guild Sovereign Seraphel Duskbane**

**Seraphel Duskbane** is the current Guild Sovereign of **Myrria’s Godshall**, and by extension the highest-ranking leader of the Adventurers’ Guild across the entire region of **Araphel** — encompassing the Shadow Steppes, Itzasoa, Ilun Tasun, and Myrria itself.

**Appearance**

Seraphel is a **fetchling**, her skin bearing the muted tones of shadow and her silver eyes glowing faintly in the dark. Wisps of shadow cling to her movements, never fully dissipating, giving her an air of quiet authority. Her silver-white hair is bound in a tight braid, and she wears the black-and-silver uniform of the guild accented with practical armor plates. A quill and a sword hang at her belt — symbols of the **Quill & Blade**.

**Background**

Seraphel’s origins are little spoken of, though most assume she was born in Myrria, where fetchlings are most commonly seen in service to the **God of Shadows**. She began her life in the guild as a **Bronze-ranked adventurer** at a remote Branch Office in the Shadow Steppes, where her affinity for twilight and darkness made her a natural scout and tracker.

Her tenacity and clever tactics against corruption cults earned her the name *Duskbane*. She rose steadily through the guild ranks, and during the eruption of the Nine Dungeons eight years ago, she coordinated the defense of Itzasoa’s river valleys, preventing the collapse of three provinces. For this feat she was elevated unanimously to **Guild Sovereign of Araphel**.

**Personality**

- **Pragmatic**: Seraphel values results over appearances. She detests needless ceremony.
- **Neutral**: True to guild law, she never involves the guild in mortal wars, though many rulers have begged or threatened her to take sides.
- **Mentor**: She carries a personal soft spot for Bronze and Silver adventurers, often reviewing their postings herself to keep them safe from bureaucratic oversight.
- **Shadow-Born Compassion**: Seraphel believes deeply in Myrria’s ethos of second chances, often taking personal interest in fugitives or exiles who seek redemption through the guild.

**Role in Myrria and Araphel**

As Guild Sovereign, Seraphel oversees all guild activity across Araphel, uniting branches from the mountain monasteries of Ilun Tasun to the steppe camps of the Shadow Steppes. She works closely with the **Council of Adventurers** who govern Myrria, ensuring that the city’s role as the **City of Second Chances** aligns with the guild’s mission.

Her leadership has given the guild an unusual prominence in Myrria, where the God of Shadows provides sanctuary but does not rule. Many citizens see her as the **mortal balance to the divine presence**.

**Notable Quirks**

- Signs every decree herself, never allowing scribes to stand in her place.
- Keeps trophies of slain monsters in the **Godshall’s tavern**, so all adventurers may see what their peers have faced.
- Known to walk the Veiled Quarter in plain clothes, listening to pilgrims and fugitives tell their stories.
