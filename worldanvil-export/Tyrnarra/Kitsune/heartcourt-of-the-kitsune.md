**The Heartcourt of the Kitsune**

The **Heartcourt** is the ruling body of the kitsune kingdom — a living polycule of between **four and nine Hearts**, each a monarch in their own right. They are bound together by **love, trust, and public acclaim**, not bloodline or conquest.

The Hearts are chosen for **charisma, wisdom, and the ability to inspire affection** in both their peers and their people. While romance often plays a role in a Heart’s ascension, so too does public trust; a Heart must be beloved by the kingdom to remain in the circle.

**The Title of Heart**

- “Heart” is both the **title of rulership** and a declaration of one’s role in the kingdom’s emotional and political life.
- Each Heart oversees a different domain — **diplomacy, defense, law, the arts, festivals, trade, faith, or magic** — though duties often overlap.
- The Heartcircle is strongest when united, but public disagreements are **common and encouraged** — they are part of governance, not a flaw in it.

**Succession and the Growing Circle**

- The Heartcourt chooses heirs **from any walk of life** — noble-born, commoner, foreigner, lover, rival, friend — anyone they believe embodies the spirit of the kingdom.
- These chosen heirs form the **Growing Circle**, living alongside the Hearts, participating in court life, and learning the duties of rulership.
- When a Heart retires, dies, or steps aside, one heir is elevated to the Heartcircle by **unanimous consent and public celebration**.

**Spectacles of Selection**

- In times of crisis or loss — especially if the Heartcircle falls below **four members** — a new Heart is chosen through **public courtship, sacred duels, or festival trials**.
- These events are **national spectacles**, blending politics, performance, and romance into multi-day celebrations.
- Competitions may test wit, beauty, or skill in equal measure — and the victor is crowned a new Heart before the gathered kingdom.

**Courtly Life**

- The Heartcourt is **famous for its drama** — romances between Hearts, lovers brought into the circle, shifting alliances, and passionate debates are all played out in public view.
- Political disputes are resolved through **open negotiation**, often accompanied by wit, poetry, or friendly competition.
- Major decisions are announced during **grand festivals**, where the people witness the Hearts’ unity in spectacle.
- A citizen may petition the Hearts directly during these gatherings — a tradition that keeps the court **rooted in the people’s voice**.

**Cultural Role**

- The Hearts are not just rulers — they are **symbols of the kingdom’s ideals**: joy, love, cleverness, beauty, and hospitality.
- To serve as a Heart is to live as both a leader and a beloved performer in the greatest play of all — the ongoing story of the kitsune people.

“A single heart may falter, but a circle of hearts beats forever.”

— Kitsune proverb

## **The Nine Hearts and Their Colors**
The Heartcourt is ideally composed of **nine Hearts**, each embodying one of the kingdom’s great pillars.

Every Heart bears a **ceremonial tail** — a richly embroidered sash in their color — worn draped from the waist or over the shoulder, signifying their domain.

When there are fewer than nine Hearts, the remaining members carry **multiple tails**, taking on the responsibilities of absent Hearts.

- The more tails a Heart wears, the greater the honor — and the heavier the burden.
- These ceremonial tails are recognized throughout the kingdom, and each is tied to centuries of tradition.

**The Nine Hearts and Their Domains**

- **Crimson Heart – Military & War**
  - **Guardian of the kingdom’s armies, defense strategies, and martial traditions.**
  - **Often the most visibly formidable member of the Heartcourt.**
  - **Symbol: A fox’s head crowned with a laurel of crimson silk.**
- **Azure Heart – Diplomacy & Foreign Relations**
  - **Oversees treaties, alliances, and the handling of foreign emissaries.**
  - **Known for charm, silver tongues, and unshakable grace under pressure.**
  - **Symbol: Two entwined fox tails over a calm wave.**
- **Golden Heart – Commerce & Wealth**
  - **Manages trade, taxation, infrastructure, and economic growth.**
  - **Balances practicality with generosity, ensuring the kingdom thrives materially.**
  - **Symbol: A fox’s paw clutching a golden coin.**
- **Emerald Heart – Nature & Stewardship**
  - **Protector of sacred groves, agricultural lands, and natural resources.**
  - **Maintains harmony between settlements and the wilds.**
  - **Symbol: A fox curled within a wreath of ivy.**
- **Ivory Heart – Law & Justice**
  - **Oversees the legal system, arbitration, and the protection of rights.**
  - **Interprets ancient oaths and codifies modern rulings.**
  - **Symbol: A fox’s mask split black and white.**
- **Violet Heart – Magic & Mysticism**
  - **Guides the use of magic, oversees magical academies, and guards the kingdom against arcane threats.**
  - **Often the most enigmatic of the Hearts.**
  - **Symbol: A fox tail wreathed in stars.**
- **Rose Heart – Arts & Festivals**
  - **Patron of music, dance, theater, and the cultural life of the kingdom.**
  - **Organizes national festivals, pageants, and public celebrations.**
  - **Symbol: A fox dancing with a ribbon.**
- **Sapphire Heart – Knowledge & Education**
  - **Oversees libraries, schools, and the preservation of history.**
  - **Seeks truth, teaches wisdom, and curates the kingdom’s cultural memory.**
  - **Symbol: An open scroll beneath a fox’s gaze.**
- **Onyx Heart – Secrets & Shadows**
  - **Keeper of intelligence networks, espionage, and covert operations.**
  - **Protects the kingdom from unseen dangers — foreign and domestic.**
  - **Symbol: A fox’s eye half-hidden in shadow.**

**The Passing of the Tails**

When a new Heart is chosen, they are **publicly presented with their Tail** during a grand festival.

- This ritual is called the **Passing of the Tails**.
- The current bearer of the Tail removes it before the gathered people and personally drapes it across the new Heart’s shoulders.
- This act is both **a transfer of authority** and a **declaration of unity**, witnessed by the kingdom.

**Ceremonial Significance**

- The Tails are made from **enchanted silk** that shimmers faintly in torchlight and moonlight, carrying the symbol associated with the color
- Some ancient Tails are centuries old, passed through dozens of Hearts.
- Losing a Tail in battle or theft is considered both a political crisis and a personal disgrace.
