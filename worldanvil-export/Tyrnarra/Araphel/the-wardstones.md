# The Wardstones

**Category:** Araphel  
**Status:** WIP  
**Updated:** 2025-09-05

**The Wardstones**

The Wardstones are seven monumental relics forged at the end of the Age of Corruption. Together they bind the god of Corruption deep beneath the earth. The stones are distributed among four realms: three kingdoms and one divine city-state.

**The Shadow Steppes**

- **The Lakeside Wardstone**: Set within a city on the shores of the great lake, this stone anchors both commerce and faith. The city grew around it as nomadic clans gathered to guard its light.
- **The Steppes Wardstone**: Standing alone in the open grasslands, this stone is protected by wandering clans. It is both a sacred pilgrimage site and a rallying point during times of war.

**Itzasoa**

- **The Forest Wardstone**: Hidden within the canopy of the great forest, it is enshrined in a vast arboreal city. Bridges of living wood and watchful druids keep eternal vigil around it.
- **The Temple Wardstone**: Set at the heart of a temple city, this stone is integrated into towering ziggurats. Priests conduct daily rites to keep corruption at bay.

**Ilun Tasun**

- **The Monastery Wardstone**: High in the mountains, monks devote their lives to guarding this stone. Pilgrims climb perilous paths to glimpse its veiled glow.
- **The Port Wardstone**: Situated in a fortified port city by the sea, this stone is both a sacred relic and a keystone of trade. The fleets of Ilun Tasun are sworn to its defense.

**Myrria**

- **The Divine Wardstone**: The last of the seven lies within Myrria, in the divine city of the God of Shadows. Unlike the others, it is openly bound to a living deity’s protection. Legends say it glows only beneath the stars and fades before the sun.
