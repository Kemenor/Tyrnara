# The Age of Corruption

**Type:** Myth  
**Category:** Base Setting  
**Status:** Draft · WIP  
**Updated:** 2025-09-05

---

*From “The Chronicles of Ash and Worm” by Sage Lorant of Highspire, 3rd Century After Binding*

“There was a time when the earth itself seemed to rot beneath our feet. The god of Corruption — whom some name the Maw Below, others the Blightfather — rose from darkness and turned his gaze upon all mortal kind. No nation was spared. Fields withered, rivers festered, and the very beasts of forest and plain were twisted into horrors unfit for this world. This time we remember as the **Age of Corruption.**

Armies crumbled, cities drowned in plague, and kingdoms were brought low. Yet amid despair, a fellowship arose — champions whose names are etched in every tongue. They bore relics of light, rallied what hosts remained, and struck into the god’s heart. They could not kill him — for such a power is beyond mortal reach — but they bound him in chains of divine law, sealed deep beneath the bones of the world. Thus ended the Age of Corruption, and so began the Counting of Years we keep today.”
