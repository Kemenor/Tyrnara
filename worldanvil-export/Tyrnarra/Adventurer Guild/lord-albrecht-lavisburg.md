# Lord Albrecht Lavisburg

**Titles:** Head of the Moral and Ethics Committee of the Adventurers’ Guild, *Minor Demi-God of Order and Ethics*

**Race:** Human

**Age:** 67

**Symbol:** A quill perfectly aligned upon a closed ledger

**Personality & Demeanor**

Lord Lavisburg is **deeply ordered and utterly devoid of fun**. He seeks perfection in all things — perfection as he defines it — and regards frivolity as a weakness to be corrected.

- Were he to sit in his immaculately maintained garden and spot a butterfly, he might remark that its wing pattern “fails to comply with authorized symmetry standards.”
- His speech is precise, with no wasted words. Every sentence feels like a formal report.
- Laughter, in his presence, is rare — and usually dies quickly when he fixes someone with his level, appraising stare.

**Role in the Guild**

As head of the **Moral and Ethics Committee**, Lavisburg oversees the codified standards of the Adventurers’ Guild:

- **Adventurer–Client Relations** (contracts, decorum, acceptable risk-taking)
- **Appropriate Conduct in Public Spaces**
- **Rules of Engagement with Civilians, Nobility, and Other Guild Members**
- **Proper Handling of Spoils, Bounties, and Artifacts**

His disciplinary tool of choice is the dreaded **ethics seminar**. The most infamous of these is:

*“Lavisburg’s Masterclass in Courteous Conduct for the Discerning Professional”*

— a multi-day ordeal of lectures, mandatory roleplay exercises, and written examinations in etiquette, delivered without a hint of irony.

**The Quirk of Belief**

Talan’s metaphysics allow **belief to shape reality** — and for adventurers, the legend of Lavisburg has grown… excessive.

- Countless tales circulate in guildhalls: how he can sense a contract violation from fifty miles away, or how his voice can stop a bar brawl mid-swing.
- Over decades, this constant storytelling — combined with his ironclad reputation — **manifested real divine power** in him.
- He is now a **minor demi-god of Order and Ethics**, though he refuses to acknowledge this title, preferring “Lord Lavisburg” or “Chairman of the Committee.”

**Divine Abilities (Belief-Forged)**

- **Aura of Compliance:** In his presence, people instinctively sit straighter, speak more politely, and avoid breaking rules.
- **Contract Binding:** Any agreement written in his sight becomes magically enforceable.
- **Perfect Recall:** He remembers every regulation, sub-clause, and precedent ever drafted.
- **Manifestation of The Review Board:** At will, he can conjure an illusory panel of spectral judges who silently observe — unnerving in disciplinary hearings.
- **Ethics Smite:** Not violent, but devastating to morale — his disappointed gaze alone can make an adventurer regret their life choices.

**Reputation Among Adventurers**

- No one *wants* to be summoned before **“The Moral and Ethics Committee Review Board under the Guidance of Lord Lavisburg”** — the phrase alone is a threat used by guild masters.
- Veterans warn new recruits to “*keep your contracts clean, your manners cleaner, and for gods’ sake, don’t eat in front of him unless you know the right fork.*”
- Ironically, his growing divine aura has only made the stories wilder, which in turn makes him even stronger.

**The Irony**

Lavisburg has never once tried to inspire fear. He is, in his own mind, simply **doing what is proper**. The fact that his presence alone can now stop drunken adventurers in mid-brawl is simply… *efficient*.
