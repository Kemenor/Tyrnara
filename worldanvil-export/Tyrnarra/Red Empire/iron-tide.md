**The Iron Tide**

**Overview**

The **Iron Tide** is the naval arm of the Red Empire, a fleet forged of black iron and fire. Its ships are not graceful; they are relentless machines of conquest, designed to crush coasts and burn ports to ash. Where the legions march over land, the Iron Tide ensures the seas carry the same fear.

The Tide embodies the Empire’s creed: mortals commanding nature through discipline and ingenuity. Its ships are powered by **fire and steam**, reinforced by **ice-bound wards** that let them cut through frozen seas and endure storms.

**Doctrine & Role**

- **Scorched Shores:** Coastal towns are annihilated before surrender; survivors are enslaved, ports rebuilt in the Empire’s image.
- **Blockade Warfare:** Entire kingdoms are strangled into submission, trade routes frozen or burned until famine forces capitulation.
- **Siege by Sea:** They specialize in bombarding cities from afar with incendiary shot, alchemical fire, and rune-bound ice bolts.
- **Relentless Pursuit:** A ship of the Iron Tide never retreats; captains are expected to scuttle their vessels rather than yield.

**Structure**

- **Admirals of Flame and Frost **– dual-command structure: one oversees incendiary divisions, the other frost-bound fleets.
- **Flamefangs **– warships armed with fire-belchers: alchemical flamethrowers, pitch-casters, and incendiary ballistae.
- **Frostfangs **– vessels clad in enchanted ice, their prows freezing seas solid to trap enemy ships.
- **Steam Hulks **– massive ironclads, driven by boilers and arcane engines. Loud, slow, unstoppable, they serve as mobile fortresses.
- **Corsair Auxilia **– captured ships crewed by conscripts or Broken, thrown into the first line of battle as sacrificial fodder.

**Arsenal**

- **Incendiary Pitch:** Thick black fire that clings to water and flesh alike.
- **Frost-Bolt Ballistae:** Runed spears of ice that pierce and freeze hulls solid.
- **Steam Cannons:** Experimental weapons that vent boiling pressure, scalding crews alive.
- **Boarding Hooks & Chains:** Once an enemy ship is crippled, boarding is swift and merciless.

**Culture & Discipline**

- Sailors of the Iron Tide are conditioned to endure both extremes: the freezing bite of frost and the searing blaze of fire.
- Life aboard is a constant trial of endurance — days in scalding boiler rooms, nights on ice-slicked decks.
- Punishment for cowardice is being lashed to the prow in freezing spray until death.
- Their creed: *“The sea is no god. It bends to iron.”*

**Reputation**

- **Inside the Empire:** The Iron Tide is revered as proof that mortal will commands even the oceans. Sailors who survive long service are honored, often elevated into the Blooded or Forged.
- **Outside the Empire:** They are feared as harbingers of annihilation. The black keels and crimson sails of the Tide mean only ruin: fire on the water, ice choking the harbors, smoke blotting the sky.
