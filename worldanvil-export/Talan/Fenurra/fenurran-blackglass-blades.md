Blackglass Blades
“Blades of Blackglass are sharper than the truth and swift as vengeance, but fragile if wielded without respect. A blade chooses its bearer and breaks in the hands of the unworthy.”

Forged from an alloy of steel and obsidian, Blackglass is uniquely Fenurran—sharper than any other weapon, capable of cutting or piercing through even the heaviest armor. But this comes at a cost: the material is brittle, and improper use—glancing blows, wild swings, or poor angle—can cause the blade to shatter.

Blackglass blades are not mass-produced. Each one is crafted for a single bearer, either by the individual (if Draconis) or in collaboration with a Draconis tribesperson. These blades contain personally selected materials—not for ritual, but for practicality and to embed personal story. Every curve, weight, and grip reflects the individual’s balance, battles, or burden. Fenurran pragmatism made them deadly; Fenurran passion made them distinct.

Two combat variants are commonly forged: sword and dagger.

Blackglass Sword
“You don’t swing it—you place it where it needs to end.”

Purpose:
Precision battlefield weapon, often used as a primary killing tool

Construction:
• Single- or double-edged blade, steel fused with obsidian
• Weighted and counterbalanced for decisive strikes
• Handle includes personal elements—bone, iron, slag-wood, volcanic resin

Function:
• Cuts through nearly any armor if used properly
• Must land clean—glancing blows risk shattering the blade
• Often carried alongside other weapons, but trusted by those trained in its limits and lethality

Wielded by:
Shock infantry, duelists, and frontline skirmishers
Marked by those who demand control, not chaos, in combat

Notes:
Sheaths are reinforced to prevent stress fractures
Some bearers etch short phrases into the spine—not for honor, but memory

Blackglass Dagger
“Your last breath will slide down it.”

Purpose:
Silent tool for assassination, infiltration, or execution

Construction:
• Obsidian-steel core, short and tapered for penetration
• Handle materials vary—burned bone, scrap iron, glassstone, wrapped leather
• Sheathed in reinforced leather or volcanic fiber

Function:
• Pierces almost any armor
• Ideal for throat cuts, joint attacks, or quiet kills
• Brittle if misused—requires steady hand and calm resolve

Wielded by:
Vexiren scouts, Brakkaun saboteurs, Draconis agents
Never issued casually—earned or made with purpose

Notes:
Each dagger carries a private engraving—a name, an insult, or a word never spoken aloud. Not a symbol. A reminder.
