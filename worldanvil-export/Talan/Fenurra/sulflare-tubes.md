# Sulflare Tubes

**Type:** Article  
**Category:** Fenurra  
**Status:** WIP  
**Updated:** 2025-07-06

---

Sulflare tubes are a combustion signal tool made from pressurized sulfur gas, Blackglass shards, and glow-ink glyphs.
Launched like a flare, it explodes into sound, light, and heat distortion.
	•	Used only at key moments—such as to signal Gatefall
	•	Said to temporarily deafen enemy archers and mark the battlefield in spirit
	•	Some believe the sound mimics the last breath of the meteor
