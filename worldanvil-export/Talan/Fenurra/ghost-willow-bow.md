Ghost Willow Bow – “Whisperfang”
    “You won’t hear the shot. You’ll feel it digging toward your breath.”
    — Vexiren archer, after the Smoke-Cut Raid

Material Origins
Fenurran bows are crafted from the rare and spectral Ghost Willow, one of the only trees capable of growing in the volcanic, sulfur-choked landscape of Fenurra. These trees are twisted, pale, and often found near Spice Wine root fields or sulfur springs. Their wood is unnaturally light, resistant to warping, and carries a faint white sheen—almost translucent in smoke-light.
    •	Bow Limbs: Ghost Willow, steam-treated and sinew-bound
    •	Bowstring: Obsidian Silk – woven from the thread of ashspinner larvae, hardened with volcanic resin. It is black beyond black, absorbing light and vanishing when drawn.
    •	When drawn, the string produces no sound, making Fenurran bows eerily quiet.

Personal Customization
Though not spiritual or ritualistic, Fenurrans are deeply passionate and individualistic. Warriors often decorate their bows with:
  •	Tassels, beads, or clipped feathers from early kills
  •	Small tokens tied to personal stories or rivalries
  •	Carvings on the grip—names, oaths, or crude jokes
