Brakkaun – The Bone-Burdened:
“We do not cast spells. We cast echoes that scatter them.”
— Rhukk-Khaz, Ashdrummer of the Broken Wall

Known as: The Bone-Burdened, The Ashdrummers, Dissonants

Core Animal Influence: Ash Boar – thick-skinned, smoke-bristled beasts that survive on what should kill

Geography:
The Brakkaun dwell in the cracked craterlands and sulfur-blasted river gorges, where even Direwolves limp from the heat. Their homes are carved into black stone and ash-glass, with ancestral bones worked into every wall and weapon.

Cultural Identity:
The Brakkaun are shamans and warriors both, the blunt edge and spiritual heart of Fenurra. Each warrior carries a bone of the fallen—not for grief, but as living vows. These bones are not mourned. They are used.

But brute force is only half their art. Brakkaun shamans are trained in the Ashdrums—massive, resonant hide-and-stone instruments bound with heat-tempered metal, carved with dissonant runes. When played in rhythm with deep-throat chants, these sounds disrupt the arcane harmonics used by the magic of the Outsiders. The effect is primal and devastating:
	•	Spells fracture mid-cast
	•	Concentration breaks
	•	Magical wards fail as if caught in an earthquake of sound

They call this power “The Shattervoice”, and they wield it with booming precision.

Combat Role:
Brakkaun warriors are the frontline and the heartbeat. They march to the beat of their shamans’ drums, break enchantments with their chants, and when needed, split skulls with bone-hilted axes and molten glaives. Many are Breaker-class riders, whose Direwolves are armored in carved obsidian and bone.

Tactics Specialty:
	•	Dissonant Warfare – magical disruption via drum-and-throat convergence
	•	Craterline Holds – heavy shield formations interwoven with chanting casters
	•	Gate-Cracking Charges – brute frontal assaults led by smoke-hardened packs

Beliefs:
	•	Pain is purpose. Weight is honor.
	•	The dead should not be buried until their task is done.
	•	Magic is not to be matched—it is to be broken.

Symbol: A cracked boar skull fused to an open mouth, tusks forming sound waves
