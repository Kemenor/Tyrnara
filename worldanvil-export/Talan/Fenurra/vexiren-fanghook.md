The Fanghook
“It is not a weapon for a fight. It is the punctuation at the end of a mistake—yours or theirs.”
— Vexiren saying

The Fanghook is less a sword and more a physical extension of the Vexiren philosophy of distance. Carried as a last resort by every archer, it is designed for a single, decisive action to correct the failure of an enemy getting within arm’s reach. It is a tool of lethal punctuation, meant to end an unwanted conversation instantly so the archer can return to their true work.

Purpose:
• Short, lightweight blade with an aggressive hooked curve, resembling a broken crescent
• Often forged from slagglass steel for its lightweight sharpness
• The hilt is minimal—usually just wrapped leather over the tang, with no crossguard to snag on a bowstring
• Sheathed horizontally at the small of the back or on the thigh for an unhindered, reverse-grip draw

Function:
• The hook is used to parry and control an opponent’s weapon, opening them for a counter-thrust
• Can hook into limbs, armor gaps, or shields, allowing the Vexiren to use an enemy’s momentum to unbalance them
• The curved blade delivers a devastating quick-draw slice or thrust
• Not a dueling blade—meant to correct the mistake of proximity and return the archer to distance

Wielded by:
All Vexiren archers. It is considered as essential a piece of their kit as their bow.
To be Vexiren is to carry a bow in your hand and a Fang at your back.
