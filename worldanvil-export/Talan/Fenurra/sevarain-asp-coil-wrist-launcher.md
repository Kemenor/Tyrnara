Asp Coil & Wrist Launcher

“I strike because you see. You fire because I move. Alone, we are swift. Together, we are certain.”
— Seravain training mantra

The standard Seravain combat pair carries identical weapons: the Asp Coil, a dual-form chainblade, and a wrist-mounted dart launcher. This mirrored toolkit allows each warrior to rotate seamlessly between offense, cover, and suppression, relying not on superior force, but on perfect rhythm and timing with their bonded partner.

Asp Coil – Modular Chainblade
Purpose: Fluid melee engagement across variable ranges
Construction:
• Core segmented spine forged from braided slagcord and obsidian links
• Edge-lined plates fold together into a rigid short blade (sword mode)
• Unlocks into a semi-flexible whipblade with forward mass (coil mode)
• Gripped from the rear handle; center shaft includes a quick-fold release

Function:
• Sword Mode: Used for precision slashes, parries, or mirrored combo strikes
• Whip Mode: Used for area sweeps, disarms, spacing strikes, or synchronized distractions
• Designed for fast stance changes mid-fight
• Often used in mirror loops—both partners whip to opposite sides to control a shared space

Wielded by:
Every Seravain frontline pair. Custom coils often feature tribal knot carvings or embedded sparkstones that leave a flare trail in whip form—especially useful for signaling in fog or ashlight.

Wrist Launcher – Mounted Dartfire Tool
Purpose: Close or mid-range ranged strikes and suppression
Construction:
• Worn on the non-dominant forearm
• Holds 2–3 pressure darts—either barbed, toxin-tipped, or piercing
• Tension-draw system reloaded manually or by partner’s support
• Triggered by knuckle press or tendon flex

Function:
• Used to create openings, interrupt reloads, or suppress magiccasters
• When one partner reloads, the other extends coverage, creating uninterrupted pressure
• Best deployed in enclosed terrain or bonded loops, where visibility is limited

Wielded by:
All bonded warriors in standard formations. Most decorate their wrist launcher with a single color line—representing the bond thread between their soul and their partner’s.
