# Prelife

## Soulwell
The origin of all Souls and Sentience. All life comes from here and ends here.

## Energy Plane
Contains an infinite sea of positive and negative energy.

## Elemental Plane
The elemental plane contains infinite regions of fire, water, wind and earth. 

# Life

## Feyworld
The plane of Chaos. Changes constantly.
## Material Plane
The plane where the continent Talan exists.
## Shadowplane
The plane of Order. Change rarely happens here.
# Postlife
The soul of the dead come 
## Elysium
The plane of Good. All Good gods reside here in their own Kingdom.
## Diyu
The plane of neutral. All Neutral gods reside here in their own Kingdom.
Domara, the judge of souls, also resides here in the City of the dead also known as Dauria.
All Souls end up here before they are picked up by god. If no god invites them to their realm, they will spend the last of their afterlife in Dauria until the soul well calls them back.
## Abyss
The Abyss contains Evil Gods, Devils and Demons.
Big City in the middle, Devils and Demons reign here.
Gods have their own Kingdoms.
