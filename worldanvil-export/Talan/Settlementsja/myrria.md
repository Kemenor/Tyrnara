# Myrria

---

Arophel the god of Darkness, resides in the city state of Myrria.
The city surrounded by mountains, there are few ways into the city, but none are large enough for an army, and thus the city state stayed mostly independent.

Once this city was known as a place for smugglers, bandits and other criminals. These days it has become a haven for freedom fighters and people looking for second chances, thanks to the efforts of an adventurer Group.
