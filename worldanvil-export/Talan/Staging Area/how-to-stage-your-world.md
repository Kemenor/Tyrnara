# What is a staging area
                        A staging area is a place for you to store and organize articles which are not completed yet or articles which are not necessarily canon to your lore.

                        These can include random inspiration, competition entries, prompt answers and ideas still in the formative stages.

                        As with all other automatically created categories, you can simply delete it if you don't  see use for it

                        

                        
> ## Pro tips
>                             1. Use the **@** followed by the name of any of your articles to easily link to it 
>                             
> 
>                             2. Use **[** followed by the name of any of your maps, timelines, categories, stats blocks or images to embed or link them into your articles.
