# The Conductor and his station

**Type:** Article  
**Category:** Other  
**Status:** Draft · WIP  
**Updated:** 2023-03-18

---

It was a simple delivery job, take the package from the earth kings capitol to frae city.
You and your party decided to take the train to the nearest harbor town and charter a ship.

It was a nice day, the rythmic rattling of the train lulled the rest of your party members to sleep already.
You weren't far behind, but so far the marvelous landscape kept you awake.
A sudden jolt rattles the train car and you feel something hit your foot, its a childrens toy.
Probably belongs to the kids from the Hobgoblin familiy, the only other passerngers in this compartment.
You bend down to pick it up, as you grasp it, it suddenly turns dark, probably just another tunnel.
But it is a little strange how dark it is, normally the magical lanterns lining the sides should have lit themselves by now.

The Tunnel ends, purple eerie light falls through the window. Instead of green rolling hills or a lush pine forest a strange starry darkness greets you from the other side.
You spot thousands of objects floating beyond, pens, toys, various suitcases, you even spot whole train cars.
Next you notice the silence, no rythmic rattling, no laughing children.
You stand up to wake you companions, when you suddenly hear one of the doors slam open, beyond only darkness and 5 purple eyes.
A grating voice pierces through the silence: "Tickets please!"
